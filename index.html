Parameter Information

  • pH: Measures water acidity or alkalinity (6.5–8.5 ideal)
  • Turbidity: Clarity of water (0–5 NTU ideal)
  • Temperature: Water temp for aquatic health (20–28°C ideal)
  • TDS: Total dissolved solids (0–500 ppm ideal)
  • Conductivity: Ionic content (0–1500 µS/cm ideal)
